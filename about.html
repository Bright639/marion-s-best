About Spike Salon

Your premier destination for beauty and grooming in Kitui Town

Style for Him. Glam for Her

At Spike Salon Barbershop & Spa, we believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best. Located in the heart of Kitui Town, we offer a comprehensive range of beauty and grooming services for both men and women in a modern, welcoming environment.

Our team of experienced professionals combines traditional barbering techniques with the latest spa treatments to provide exceptional service that exceeds expectations. Whether you're looking for a classic haircut, professional nail services, or a relaxing spa treatment, we have you covered.

Our Service Areas

Comprehensive beauty and grooming solutions under one roof

Barbershop

Traditional and modern barbering services including classic cuts, beard trimming, clean shaves, and dreadlock maintenance by skilled barbers.

Salon

Professional hairdressing services including styling, coloring, treatments, and special occasion hair design for all hair types.

Manicure & Pedicure

Complete nail care services including manicures, pedicures, gel applications, nail art, and nail health treatments in a hygienic environment.

Massage

Therapeutic and relaxation massages including Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone treatments, and aromatherapy in our tranquil spa environment.

Facials

Customized facial treatments for all skin types including deep cleansing, anti-aging, hydrating, and specialized treatments for healthy, glowing skin.

Specialized Services

Premium services including microblading, eyelash extensions, wigging, waxing, steam treatments, and Moroccan bath experiences.
